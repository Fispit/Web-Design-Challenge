Maximum Temperature




Temperature is the variable that is most directly affected by latitude. The main driving force of temperature is the average insolation and the higher the latitude goes the during winter the harsher the temperature drop is. This effect is best seen during winter when the total insolation of a hemisphere is reduced due to the angle of the earth showing a lower area profile towards the sun.
